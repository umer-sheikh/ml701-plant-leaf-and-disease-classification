About Project

Accurate plant species identification is crucial for various fields, from ecological research to landscape maintenance. However, the expertise required for precise identification often limits accessibility for non-specialists. This project aims to bridge this gap by developing a user-friendly "Simplified Plant Species Identification" application, harnessing the power of deep learning models to facilitate accurate plant recognition for a broader audience.

The project will utilize a comprehensive image dataset, such as Leafsnap or PlantVillage, encompassing a wide range of plant species, to train a deep learning model for effective species recognition and classification. These datasets provide extensive visual information, including leaves, flowers, and fruits, enabling the trained model to deliver precise species identification.

To enhance user experience and accessibility, an online portal will be developed. Users can simply upload an image of an unknown plant, and the model will process the image, identify the corresponding species, and provide the botanical name. Additionally, if time permits, the application will offer care instructions tailored to the identified plant species.
